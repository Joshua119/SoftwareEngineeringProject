Background

Through their learning careers, students will have to rely on typing in order to take notes and complete various assignments. Our goal is to create an educational game that will train students to become proficient typers. Additionally, we hope to improve their knowledge of social studies by teaching them about American history and geography.

Project Description

In this game students will be able to explore a virtual recreation of a colonial town and interact with its inhabitants. By interacting with the NPCs, stdents can also play three different mini-games that will test their typing skills and knowledge of social studies. Our three mini-games will be a typing racer, a vocabulary game and a quiz on geography. This game is recommended for students in grades 4 through 6.
